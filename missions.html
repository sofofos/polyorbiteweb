Spacebean

SpaceBean is an incubator designed to study the viability of plant growth in a microgravity environment. This project will test a new soil conditioning and provide data for a model of legumes in microgravity. It is a self-sufficient and autonomous system that will be able to determine the best conditions for the growth of the plant.

SACHS

Sachs is an onboard computer with the capacity to reprogram itself autonomously. It provides a new solution to process and store information in space and avoids data corruption due to cosmic radiation. It is designed to be self-adaptive for long journey in outer space. To this end, it has the ability to constantly check for errors in its own system and self-correct itself by changing its architecture and key components. This gives to the satellite a faster response time when malfunction and change in the environment appear making it less susceptible mission failure.
